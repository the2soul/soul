About Our Farm

Our Story

Golden Feather Poultry Farm was established in 2005 with just 100 chickens and a vision to provide healthy, sustainable poultry products to our community. Today, we operate a 50-acre farm with state-of-the-art facilities while maintaining our commitment to ethical farming practices.

Our Farming Process

  1. Breeding: We select the healthiest breeds for optimal egg production and meat quality.
  2. Hatching: Eggs are hatched in our modern, climate-controlled incubators.
  3. Rearing: Chicks are raised in temperature-controlled brooder houses for the first few weeks.
  4. Free-Range Growth: Chickens are moved to free-range pens with access to outdoor areas.
  5. Harvesting: Eggs are collected daily, and chickens are processed humanely at our on-site facility.
  6. Distribution: Products are packaged and delivered fresh to customers within 24 hours.

Our Poultry Products

Fresh Eggs

Daily collected, free-range eggs with rich yolks and strong shells. Available in various sizes:

  • Small (18oz per dozen)
  • Medium (21oz per dozen)
  • Large (24oz per dozen)
  • Extra Large (27oz per dozen)
  • Jumbo (30oz per dozen)

Chicken Meat

Premium quality chicken raised without antibiotics or growth hormones:

  • Whole Chickens (3-8 lbs)
  • Chicken Breasts (boneless/skinless)
  • Chicken Thighs & Drumsticks
  • Chicken Wings

All our chickens are processed in our USDA-approved facility and vacuum-packed for freshness.
